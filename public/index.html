🚀 How to Start a Dispute

1

Upload or Select a Document

Choose a medical bill or EOB that you want to dispute. You can upload a new document or select from previously analyzed documents.

2

Review Detected Errors

Our AI will automatically scan your document for common billing errors like overcharging, duplicate charges, coding errors, and more.

3

Generate Dispute Letter

For each detected error, we'll generate a professional dispute letter tailored to your specific situation and document.

4

Review and Submit

Review the generated letter, make any edits if needed, and submit your dispute. We'll track the progress for you.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Start with high-confidence errors: These are most likely to result in successful disputes
  • Keep evidence organized: Save copies of all correspondence and responses
  • Follow up regularly: Most providers respond within 30 days
  • Be persistent: Don't give up if your first dispute is denied
